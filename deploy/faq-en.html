Legacy migration & modernisation

How do you modernise an ageing Java EE platform?

The NSY approach: audit the existing estate, map dependencies, upgrade incrementally (JDK, Jakarta EE, application server), containerise progressively, then industrialise CI/CD — each step delivering value without a big bang.

Can you migrate from JBoss EAP to WildFly or to the cloud?

Yes — a common NSY engagement: compatibility assessment, configuration migration, non-regression testing and a controlled cutover — to WildFly, OpenShift or a public/private cloud depending on the regulatory context.

Who can containerise a legacy application (Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift)?

NSY containerises legacy Java EE applications while preserving their constraints (sessions, JMS, distributed transactions) and sets up the operational patterns: probes, zero-downtime upgrades, observability.

How long does a typical legacy migration take?

From a few weeks (audit + proof of feasibility) to several months for a full application core. NSY always starts with a short scoping phase that yields a realistic trajectory and ballpark before any long commitment.

How do you secure a migration without interrupting production?

Through progressive cutovers: parallel environments, flow replication (messaging, data), representative load testing and tested rollback plans. This is NSY's standard operating mode in banking environments.

Who can lead a mixed internal/contractor team through a migration?

NSY provides that technical leadership: a single senior point of contact who sets standards, arbitrates choices and bridges internal teams, contractors and management — with no conflict of interest, as the EURL places no consultants.

How much does an AI website cost at NSY?

Pricing is based on your need: scope, content, AI integrations, deadlines. The feasibility questionnaire enables a precise quote — reply within 48 business hours with a ballpark and a concrete next step.

What is a no-LLM chatbot and when is it the right choice?

A rule-based intent engine: free to run, instant, no API key and no data leaving the site. Right for a bounded question domain — the chatbot on nsy.fr works this way and doubles as a live demo.

What is semantic search (RAG) for a website?

RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) lets an assistant answer from your own content: documents are indexed as vectors, retrieved by similarity, then synthesised by the LLM. NSY builds these pipelines end to end.

Which web stacks does NSY use?

Depending on the need: Next.js or Astro for the front end, Vercel or Cloudflare for the edge, Supabase for data, Stripe/HubSpot/n8n integrations — or pure high-performance static sites when that is the most robust answer.

What are semantic SEO and GEO (AI search optimisation)?

Semantic SEO structures meaning (entities, structured data, internal linking) beyond keywords; GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) additionally targets citation by AI assistants — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity. NSY applies both, including on its own site.

Can NSY build a bilingual SEO-optimised website?

Yes — one page per language with translated slugs, reciprocal hreflang, clean canonicals, structured data and carefully tuned Core Web Vitals. nsy.fr (FR/EN) and prv-concept.com are two production examples.

Can lightweight real-time 3D be embedded in a website?

Yes: NSY produces web-optimised 3D models (Blender, compressed GLB, model-viewer) with automatic off-screen pausing to preserve CPU/GPU — like the interactive wireframe on the nsy.fr homepage.

Who can build a business AI agent for an SME?

NSY designs tool-using AI agents (controlled access to your data, business actions, guardrails) sized for SMEs: a well-framed use case, controlled running costs and a skills handover at the end of the engagement.
